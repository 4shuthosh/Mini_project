About Zilupe

City Facts

  • Population: Approximately 2,300
  • Region: Latgale Region
  • Established: First mentioned in 1533
  • Geographic Location: Eastern Latvia, near Russian border

Historical Highlights

Zilupe is a small town with rich historical significance, located in the easternmost part of Latvia. It's known for its border location and unique cultural heritage.
